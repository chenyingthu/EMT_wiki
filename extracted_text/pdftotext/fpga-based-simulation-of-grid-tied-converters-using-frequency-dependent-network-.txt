                                                               FPGA, achieving sub-microsecond latencies, low resource utilization, and high computational ﬁdelity across var-
                                                               ious data types, including single-, double-precision, and customized ﬂoating-point formats. The numerical test
                                                               and validation were conducted using a high-voltage power network that includes detailed models of transmis-
                                                               sion lines, loads, and a Static Synchronous Compensator (STATCOM), etc. Simulation results show strong align-
                                                               ment with reference models developed in the EMTP, achieving faster-than-real-time performance. These ﬁndings
                                                               demonstrate the eﬀectiveness of the proposed solution in delivering high-speed, resource-eﬃcient, and scalable
                                                               real-time simulations, providing a promising approach for testing and validating advanced control strategies in
                                                               modern power systems.
