                 V. PASSIVITY ENFORCEMENT
   If the fitted formulation shows passivity violation, its param-
eters can be modified so as to ensure positive eigenvalues for
       . A nonlinear optimization algorithm can be used for this
purpose, which penalizes negative eigenvalue excursions, but
such an algorithm can be numerically inefficient and susceptible
to non-convergence.

strained least squares algorithm can be used to perturb the fitted
parameters to achieve passivity. However, such a modification
