B. Path-Based Partial Refactorization
   The path-based approach aims to minimize the subset of
columns that need refactoring in the left-looking algorithm.
For each changed value in the original matrix, it computes a                    Fig. 1. Left, sparse pattern of a U matrix (non-zeros in gray), right, its graph

factors. Its computation involves determining column depen-

applied to partial refactorization.
