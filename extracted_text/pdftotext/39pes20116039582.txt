   For EHV network modeling, a Joint CIGRE/CIRED WG
CC02 document [6] recommends to model accurately at
least the complete primary transmission network.
Furthermore it is appropriate to consider the loads on the
secondary transmission network in order to decide whether
these should be modeled explicitly or as an equivalent
circuit. If these loads are placed directly on the secondary
side of the transformer, the damping they cause can be
overestimated when using simple equivalents.
   As a consequence to accurately assess the harmonic
impedance close to new EHV cable installation, it is needed
to model at least the 400 kV French grid.
