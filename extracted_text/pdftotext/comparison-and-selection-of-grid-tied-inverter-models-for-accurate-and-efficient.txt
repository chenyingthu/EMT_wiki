   This article focuses on the modeling methods for existing
EMT simulation programs equipped with a conventional algo-
rithm based on the nodal analysis and fixed time-step simulation.

often a large-scale network including multiple inverters. Even
if the network becomes large and complex, the nodal analysis
can simply formulate the network according to a prescribed
procedure, while the computational effort to generate state-space
equations becomes excessive in the state-space analysis [28]. In
addition, the variable time-step method [29]–[31] cannot effec-
