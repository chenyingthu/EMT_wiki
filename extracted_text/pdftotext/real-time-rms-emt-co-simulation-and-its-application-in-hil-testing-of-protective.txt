Real-time simulation                                           in simulation platforms to perform Root Mean Square (RMS)- Electromagnetic Transient (EMT) real-time, multi-

Hardware-in-the-loop

Protective relay

                                                               have similar built-in transmission line models. To convert waveforms to phasors, a non-buffered rapid curve

                                                               highlight the benefits of the proposed interfacing technique.
