Junhua Xu * , Jiyuan Shen , Xinyu You , Chunwei Wang , Bin Liu
School of Electrical Engineering, Guangxi University, located in Nanning, China

Keywords:                                                   To enhance the accuracy of dynamic equivalent models for wind farms utilizing doubly-fed induction generators
DFIG wind farms                                             (DFIGs), an enhanced k-means two-step clustering method for dynamic equivalent modeling of DFIG wind farms
Dynamic equivalent modeling                                 based on low-voltage ride-through (LVRT) characteristics is proposed in this study. First, the entire turbine fleet
LVRT characteristics
                                                            is divided into two categories based on their active power output and LVRT response: those exhibiting clustering
Two-step clustering
Enhanced k-means clustering
                                                            characteristics and those that do not. For the latter, an enhanced k-means algorithm is applied. This approach
                                                            optimizes initial center selection through probabilistic selection, improves search efficiency by utilizing a (K-
                                                            Dimensional Tree) KD tree (reducing data search time by approximately 75% compared to traditional k-means),
                                                            and determines the ideal cluster count using the Davies-Bouldin Index (DBI). The method overcomes the limi-
                                                            tations of existing algorithms that require manual specification of initial centers and cluster numbers. Simulation
                                                            results demonstrate that the equivalent model accurately corresponds to the detailed model during faults.
                                                            Notably, the simulation time is reduced by about 90% while maintaining high precision, providing an efficient
                                                            and accurate modeling approach for power system stability analysis with high wind power penetration.
