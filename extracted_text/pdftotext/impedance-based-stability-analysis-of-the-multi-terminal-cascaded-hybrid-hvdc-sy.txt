   Abstract—The cascaded hybrid high voltage direct current
(HVDC) combines the strengths of the line commutated converter
(LCC) and the modular multilevel converter (MMC) in the long dis-
tance large capacity power transmission. However, its distributed
muti-terminal grid structure raises the complexity of the whole
system drastically. To clarify the oscillation mechanism of the multi-
terminal cascaded hybrid HVDC system, utilizing the merits of the
impedance model, this paper proposes an impedance based stability
analysis method, which decomposes stability analysis problem into
a hierarchical structure. In addition, an equivalent single-input                  Fig. 1.    Layout of the multi-terminal cascaded hybrid HVDC system.
