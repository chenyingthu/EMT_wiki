   Frequency-Domain Simulation of Electromagnetic Transients Using Variable

                               Abner Ramirez, Senior Member, IEEE, and Reza Iravani, Fellow, IEEE


   Abstract—This letter presents the extension of a previously
proposed frequency-domain (FD) approach for the analysis of
electromagnetic transients (EMTs), primarily due to a set of
discrete switching events, in an electric power network. The
method is based on dividing the analysis time window into a set of
subwindows with equal or unequal time lengths and solving each
in the FD. The sampling time step is also independently selected
for each subwindow. The extended method, formerly proposed for
a single transmission line, can readily use the concept of network
equivalents, achieving efﬁcient EMT solutions of large systems,
and can simultaneously accommodate slow and fast dynamics.
