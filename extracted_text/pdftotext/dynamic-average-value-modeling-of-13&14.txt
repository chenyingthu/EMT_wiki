                                                                                                  III. DYNAMIC AVM
                                                                               In this paper, the AVM is developed in both Matlab/Simulink
                                                                            and PSCAD/EMTDC. In order to model a complex system,
                                                                            such as the CIGRE HVDC benchmark, it is necessary to divide
                                                                            it up into smaller parts. Hence, the main circuit of HVDC
                                                                            system is divided into seven subsystems: two line-commutated
                                                                            converters (rectifier and inverter sides), which are modeled
                                                                            using the parametric AVM approach; two ac subsystems; two
                                                                            controller subsystems; and the dc transmission-line subsystem.
                                                                            For convenience, each subsystem is implemented as a separate
                                                                            module. These modules are then interconnected to form the
                                                                            overall HVDC system.
