based on the solution of full-wave Maxwell’s equations, and it can
consider any type of tower, including towers whose height is compara­

    To assess the occurrence of a BF across the string of insulators, the
existing models that correlate the time-domain overvoltage and time-to-
flashover can be employed that include the volt-time curve method [19,
20], the disruptive effect (DE) model [21–25], and the leader progres­
sion (LP) model [4,26,27]. Here, a backflashover analysis is performed
using the first lightning strokes measured at two different stations,
namely, Visacro et al. [28] and Anderson and Eriksson [29], and
employing the DE and LP methods.
