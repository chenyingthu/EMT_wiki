   Abstract—Multi-rate Electromagnetic Transient (EMT) simu-
lations use smaller time-steps for parts of the network requiring
greater accuracy, and larger time-steps for the rest of the network.
This paper presents an analytical approach for evaluating the
stability of multi-rate EMT simulations of linear time-invariant
(LTI) networks. It is shown that their resulting discrete time sys-
tem is inherently time-periodic. Leveraging this characteristic, a
sampled-data time-invariant representation of the simulated net-
work is derived. The overall simulation’s numerical stability can
then be assessed through eigenvalue analysis. The paper shows
