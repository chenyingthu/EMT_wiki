## 摘要

Based on vector decoupling control strategy, an electromechanical transient model of direct drive permanent magnet synchronous generator (PMSG) system, in which the structure of twin back-to-back converters is utilized and the most common space vector decoupling control scheme is adopted, is built. The built model is suitable for power system transient stability analysis. Two reasonable hypotheses for the built model are made: active power and reactive power are fully decoupled during the transient process and the measured electrical quantities are accurate and fully equal to actual ones.
