## 摘要

1: High level of modular multilevel converter (MMC) restrains electromagnetic transient (EMT) simulation speed. To address this issue, an MMC fast model based on time-scale-frame transformation (TSFT) is proposed in this paper. The key feature of TSFT is to construct an analytical signal of single-phase real signal and perform coordinate frame rotation transformation to reduce the frequency of original signal utilizing rotation characteristics of analytical signal, thereby large step simulation can be achieved. Because of single-phase transformation, the TSFT avoids frequency multiplication problem in the case of three-phase asymmetry happening on Park transformation.
