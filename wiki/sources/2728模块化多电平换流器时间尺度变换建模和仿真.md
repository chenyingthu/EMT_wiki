## 摘要

1: High level of modular multilevel converter (MMC) restrains electromagnetic transient (EMT) simulation speed. To address this issue, an MMC fast model based on time-scale-frame transformation (TSFT) is proposed in this paper. The key feature of TSFT is to construct an analytical signal of single-phase real signal and perform coordinate frame rotation transformation to reduce the frequency of original signal utilizing rotation characteristics of analytical signal, thereby large step simulation can be achieved. Because of single-phase transformation, the TSFT avoids frequency multiplication problem in the case of three-phase asymmetry happening on Park transformation. ## 主要发现



- 仿真结果与PSCAD详细模型高度吻合，验证了时间尺度变换模型的动态响应准确性
- 该大步长模型显著减少计算节点与矩阵规模，大幅提升MMC电磁暂态仿真速度
- 单相旋转变换有效避免三相不对称工况下的负序倍频问题，保证复杂工况仿真精度
