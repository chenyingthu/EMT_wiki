## 摘要

The objective of this two-part paper is to provide clarity to physical concepts used in the field of transformer mod- eling, to dispel common misconceptions regarding numerical in- stabilities, and to present unified modeling techniques for low- frequency transients. The paper focuses on proper modeling of nonlinearities (magnetizing branches), since these components are critical to determine the low-frequency behavior. A good low- frequency model should properly represent: normal operation, inrush currents, open and short circuit, out-of-phase synchroniza- tion transient of step-up transformers, geomagnetic induced cur- rents, ferroresonance, and harmonics. ## 核心贡献


- 基于对偶原理推导统一变压器拓扑模型，适配不同铁芯结构以覆盖全低频暂态工况
- 对比磁阻网络法与直接对偶法，证明双侧对偶模型具有更强通用性与物理准确性
- 证明负电感、互感耦合与BCTRAN漏磁模型数学等效，澄清负电感非数值振荡根源

## 主要发现


- 双侧对偶模型比单侧更具通用性，可统一表征变压器在不同工况下的低频暂态响应
- 负电感、互感耦合与BCTRAN模型数学等效，均能准确等效多绕组变压器漏磁通
- 仿真验证表明数值振荡源于铁芯拓扑模型构建错误，而非负电感元件本身导致
