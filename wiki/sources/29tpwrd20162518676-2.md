## 摘要

—Strategic power ﬂow control using a Sen Trans- former (ST) can be a robust and cost-effective solution to relieve grid congestion due to increased installation of renewables. The ST consists of a multi-winding transformer and tap changer that can regulate the power ﬂow through a transmission line by injecting a series connected controllable voltage. This paper develops a real-time high-ﬁdelity magnetic equivalent circuit (MEC) based electromagnetic transient (EMT) model for the ST on the ﬁeld programmable gate array (FPGA) for hardware- in-the-loop (HIL) applications. This geometry-based model was developed to depict the major ﬂux paths in the transformer core, and complex nonlinear phenomena such as saturation, hysteresis and eddy currents. ## 主要发现



- FPGA模型实现微秒级步长实时仿真，结果与三维有限元软件JMAG高度吻合
- 全并行流水线架构显著降低仿真延迟与硬件资源占用，满足32位浮点精度要求
- 模型有效克服分段线性近似的数值振荡问题，精确复现磁滞与涡流暂态过程
