## 摘要

Digital electromagnetic transient simulation is an important way to analyze the operation, planning and control of power system. With the large capacity flexible DC transmission, flexible AC transmission, micro-network and renewable energy accessed to power grid in the further application, power system presents the trend of flexible electric power electronic, traditional digital electromagnetic transient simulation technology is unable to meet the research, experiment, production and other needs. This paper aims to review the research of efficient digital electromagnetic transient simulation technology at home and abroad in recent years, discuss the key technologies to enhance the simulation efficiency, extract the existing problems and difficulties, and clarify the next research and devel

## 核心贡献



- 系统综述了近年来国内外高效数字电磁暂态仿真技术的研究现状
- 从算法、硬件、接口和模型四个维度提炼了提升仿真效率的关键技术
- 指出了当前技术面临的瓶颈与难点，并明确了未来的研发方向

## 主要发现



- 传统电磁暂态仿真难以满足高比例电力电子化电网的大规模仿真需求
- 并行计算架构、新型硬件加速与混合仿真接口是突破算力瓶颈的核心路径
- 快速等效模型与平均化建模技术能显著提升仿真速度但需兼顾精度与无源性
- 多时间尺度协同与标准化接口协议是未来高效仿真技术发展的重点方向
