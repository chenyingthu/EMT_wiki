## 摘要

To accurately and efficiently reflect the operation characteristics of power electronic dominated power system under the disturbance of the parameter random migration, the power system dynamic model based on stochastic differential equation and its numerical solution algorithms were analyzed firstly in terms of basic characteristic and limitations. Then, regarding the basic electrical components, the dynamic companion circuit model considering the random excitation of the parameter was established for electromagnetic transient simulation.
