## 摘要

Multi-scale modeling for accurate, fast and flexible simulation of electromagnetic and electromechanical transients in voltage source converter-based high voltage direct current (VSC-HVDC) transmission systems was presented and validated. It makes use of the methodologies such as shifted frequency analysis (SFA). A novel analytical relationship between the shifted frequency (SF)-domain and dq-domain was derived leading to a system-level SFA-based VSC model. Additionally, a multi-scale model of DC transmission lines was developed with selective insertion of a -segment. By adjusting the simulation parameters such as time-step size, diverse transients with multiple time-scales and in different network-positions can be simulated. - 推导移频域与dq域解析变换关系，构建系统级SFA-VSC多尺度模型

- 通过调节时间步长与移频参数，实现多时间尺度暂态的灵活统一仿真

## 主要发现



- 模型精确复现高频电磁暂态，同时大幅降低慢变化机电暂态的计算成本
- 两端与五端系统算例验证了模型对接地故障及风电波动的仿真准确性
- 调节仿真参数有效提升了电磁与机电暂态混合仿真的灵活性与接口精度
