# A Novel Substation Back-up Protection Based on Communication Channel of Pilot Protection

**年份**: 2013
**来源**: `03/PESMG.2013.6672207.pdf.pdf`

## 摘要

In order to solve the problems of traditional distance protection when it is used as back-up protection, a novel substa­ tion back-up protection scheme based on communication channel of pilot protection is proposed. According to comprehensive deci­ sion-making result of action states of protections in local substa­ tion and fault detection information transmitted by adjacent sub­ stations, the functions of proposed substation back-up protection are realized. Finally, a simulation model of IEEE lO-generator- 39-node system is built in PSCAD/EMTDC to test the perfor­ mance of proposed substation back-up protection. ## 核心贡献


- 提出利用导引保护光纤通道传输辅助信息的变电站后备保护新方案

- 避免依赖广域通信系统，提升保护信息同步性与工程应用可靠性
