# Earth Return Impedance of Overhead and Underground Conductors Considering Earth Stratification

**作者**: 未知
**年份**: 2008
**来源**: `13&14/files/TPWRS.2008.923816.pdf.pdf`

## 摘要

The influence of earth stratification on the conductor impedances is investigated. A general solution of the electromagnetic-field equations for the case of overhead and underground transmission lines is presented.

## 核心贡献

- 建立了考虑频率相关特性的transmission-line模型，提高了暂态仿真精度
- 考虑了设备参数的频率相关特性，提高宽频暂态分析精度
