# 分布式光伏电源分散式自适应主动频率支撑控制

**作者**: dc.cqvip.com

## 摘要

With the explosive growth of distributed photovoltaic (PV) penetration, the traditional passive access method mainly based on maximum power point tracking (MPPT) increasingly threatens the frequency security and stability of the system. The demand for active support for system frequency by distributed PV sources becomes increasingly urgent. The existing active support strategies require online estimation of their maximum available power, which is a large computation burden and makes it difficult to adapt to rapid changes in environmental conditions. Because of this, a quantitative characterization parameter is proposed, which represents the real-time output power of the PV system as a ratio to the current maximum available power, thus avoiding the online estimation of the maximum available

## 核心贡献



- 提出了一种量化表征参量（实时出力与当前最大可用功率之比），避免了最大可用功率的在线估计，降低了计算负担并适应环境快速变化
- 设计了分散式自适应主动频率支撑控制策略，使并联分布式光伏能根据频率变化自适应调整功率表征参量，等比例调控储备功率以主动响应频率扰动
