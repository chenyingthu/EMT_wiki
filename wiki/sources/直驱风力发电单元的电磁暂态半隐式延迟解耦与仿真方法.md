## 摘要

Due to the large scale of existing wind farms, the number of wind turbines and the difficulty of modeling and simulation of wind turbines, the efficiency of electromagnetic transient simulation (EMT) of wind farms is low. In order to solve the above problems, a fine-grained decoupling and parallel simulation method for direct-drive wind power generation unit was proposed based on the semi-implicit latency decoupling (SILDP) method to realize efficient EMT of direct-drive wind power generation unit. Firstly, based on the matrix splitting principle, the direct-drive wind power generation unit was divided into several subsystems according to the state variables.
