## 摘要

—This paper approaches the solution of electromag- netic transients in the frequency domain from the point of view of choosing a time window with sufﬁcient width to allow the time constants of the transients to decay to a small value. This criterion, and the required maximum frequency content of the simulation, fully determine the simulation parameters. Satisfying this criterion, a very simple methodology, like the Discrete Time Fourier Series can provide very accurate results without the inherent difﬁculties of time-warping solutions based on the Numerical Laplace Transform (NLT). Even though the NLT can give very accurate results, it requires expert knowledge to correctly specify the damping factors, sampling schemes, and windowing functions.
